HOW THE MEGHTERBIN MEJTEMIIN GLOBAL NETWORK WORKS

Megtherbin Mejtemiin (or United Diaspora) is a decentralized network of the Lebanese diaspora across the world with the sole objective of establishing and maintaining a civil, secular, and democratic Lebanon while supporting the Oct 17th revolution demands.

    The network is largely made up of volunteers from around the world, organized into 2 categories:
  • City chapters and representatives who organize and meet with expats in their locales and then nominate
  • Voting members on MM’s committees to ensure international coordination and collaboration on network priorities
