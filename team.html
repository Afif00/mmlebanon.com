INTERNATIONAL COORDINATION TEAM

The International Coordination team consists of 10 elected Chairs and Vice-Chairs who lead their respective committees within the network.

Their main responsibilities are to facilitate and coordinate the work amongst the different committees as well as represent their committee when voting on decisions that impact the network.

There are 5 committees in Meghterbin Mejtemiin: the Political Committee, the Networking Committee, the Legal Committee, the Impact & Development Committee and the Communications Committee.
