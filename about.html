About

We Are A Global Movement

We are Meghterbin Mejtemiin, مغتربین مجتمعین , Diaspora Unie, United Diaspora.

We are a global network of Lebanese diaspora who strive to establish and maintain an independent, civil, secular, and democratic Lebanese state that prioritizes the welfare of the people within its borders and its unified diaspora.

Founded on October 18, 2019, we work to activate and mobilize a unified Lebanese diaspora in tandem with the October 17 revolution demands. We support and facilitate activities, initiatives, and direct/long-term action towards social, economic, environmental, racial, political, labor, and gender justice in Lebanon.

Vision

We strive to establish and maintain an independent, civil, secular, and democratic Lebanese state that prioritizes the welfare of the people within its borders and its unified diaspora.

Mission

We work to activate and mobilize a unified Lebanese diaspora in tandem with the October 17 revolution demands.

Our values

Meghterbin Mejtemiin supports and facilitates activities and initiatives towards social, economic, political and environmental justice.

We stand firmly with:

  • Outing of the sectarian and classist system in all of its forms and institutions
  • Abolishing the sectarian and patriarchal society which oppresses women, the LGBTQ+ community, people with disability, migrants, refugees and working-class citizens.

We support and work towards:

  • Implementing secularism at all levels of governance.
  • Imposing pure legal reform and independent judiciary.
  • Unifying the diaspora/immigrants/expats by collective activism.

We work inter-sectionally and believe in the interconnectedness of multiple struggles, such as but not limited to the following:

  • We view racism in Lebanon as embedded in the daily struggle of refugees and migrant workers for dignity and basic rights.
  • We refuse the instrumentalization of Palestinian and Syrian refugees for political leverage and the continuous violation of their human rights and dignity.
  • We support the Palestinian cause and their movement towards freedom and civil rights.

Our aims

  • Activate the Lebanese communities in all major international cities and become the focal point to all outgoing immigrants.
  • Harness a culture of collaboration and shared resources within the network and its partners inside and outside of Lebanon.
  • Identify and meet the needs of the diaspora as well as local communities
  • Capitalize on the Lebanese diaspora’s resources and capabilities.
  • Establish and maintain an independent, civil, secular, and democratic Lebanese state.
  • Secure the welfare of the people within its borders and its unified diaspora.
  • Fight for social, economic, environmental, racial, political, labor, and gender justice in Lebanon.
  • Hold the government, the president and the parliament accountable for the gruesome crimes committed against the Lebanese people.
