Manifesto

We are the revolution abroad

We are Meghterbin Mejtemiin, a global network of Lebanese diaspora who strive to establish and maintain an independent, civil, secular, and democratic Lebanese state that prioritizes the welfare of the people within its borders and its unified diaspora. We refuse to accept that nepotism, favoritism, and sectarianism are innate to our Lebanese culture.
Founded on October 18, 2019, we work to activate and mobilize a unified Lebanese diaspora in tandem with the October 17 revolution demands. We support and facilitate activities, initiatives, and direct/long-term action towards social, economic, environmental, racial, political, labor, and gender justice in Lebanon.
